Our Story

Computer Repair Doctor’s local store in Raleigh NC was established in 2015. Our founder & CEO Matt Ham grew up in Raleigh and spent several years living in the north Raleigh / midtown area. He knew the market would be great for a CRD store, and so after opening a few stores in Florida the company decided to grow to the Raleigh area and expand their iPhone and laptop repair services to central NC.

Aaron Schoeffler (the company’s COO) moved from Tallahassee to Raleigh to spearhead the store’s opening and oversee growth in the area. CRD’s Raleigh store was open on Falls of the Neuse Rd in the midtown/north Raleigh area and has been doing well ever since!
